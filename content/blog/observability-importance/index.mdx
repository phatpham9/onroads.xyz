Observability is a crucial aspect of software development. It allows developers to monitor and understand the internal state of a system by examining its outputs. Observability is important because it helps in identifying and resolving issues quickly, improving system performance, and ensuring high-quality software delivery.

In this blog post, we will discuss the importance of observability, its benefits, and how it can improve software quality and performance.
